About Ancient Aroma

Welcome to ANCIENT AROMA, where every cup tells a story! Nestled in the heart of the city, our premium café offers a cozy escape with an inviting ambiance and expertly crafted beverages.

Our journey began with a simple passion for quality coffee. Today, we pride ourselves on sourcing the finest ethically harvested beans from around the world, roasted to perfection to bring out their unique flavors and aromas.

Whether you're looking for a quiet spot to unwind or a vibrant space to connect with friends, Ancient Aroma is your go-to destination. Join us for a delightful experience that awakens your senses and inspires your day!

The Ancient Aroma Story

Ancient Aroma didn't begin in a café — it started in the minds of three first-year B.Tech students from Chitkara University, batch of 2024. Harshit Mehta, Harshit Gatyan, and Harshit Hassija weren't just united by their names, but by their shared love for coffee, code, and creativity.

What began as a frontend development project quickly evolved into something more meaningful — a full-on digital café management interface, inspired by real-world challenges and driven by a passion to learn and innovate. The trio worked tirelessly, combining web development, UI/UX design, and creative storytelling into a project that felt as real as any business out there.

While the café may be fictional for now, the experience, learning, and dedication behind Ancient Aroma are 100% real. From animations and interactivity to backend logic and user-focused design, every part of this project reflects their journey as aspiring developers.

In a world full of ideas, Ancient Aroma is proof that even the smallest concept — when brewed with passion — can become something special. This is more than just a website. It's the start of three developers building their dreams, one line of code at a time.

The story continues — with new features, better design, and more innovation on the way. And just like a great cup of coffee, it's only getting better with time.

Crafted by 1st Year B.Tech Students | Chitkara University | Batch of 2024
