Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer free WiFi?

Yes, we offer complimentary high-speed WiFi at all our locations. Simply ask our staff for the password.

Are your cafes pet-friendly?

Yes, well-behaved pets on leashes are welcome in our outdoor seating areas. Service animals are welcome throughout our cafes.

Do you offer vegan and gluten-free options?

We have a variety of vegan and gluten-free options on our menu. Please inform our staff about any dietary restrictions or allergies.

Can I book a table in advance?

Yes, you can book a table through our website, by phone, or using our mobile app. Reservations are recommended for groups of 4 or more.

Do you cater for private events?

Yes, we offer catering services and can host private events at select locations. Please contact us at events@ancientaroma.com for more information.
