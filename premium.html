Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our Premium Card

How soon will I receive my Premium Card after applying?

After your application is approved, you'll receive a digital card immediately via email. If you've selected a plan that includes a physical card, it will be mailed to you within 5-7 business days.

Can I cancel my membership at any time?

Yes, you can cancel your membership at any time. Monthly plans can be canceled before the next billing cycle. Annual plans are non-refundable but will remain active until the end of your paid term.

How do I redeem my Premium Card benefits?

Simply present your physical card or digital card (via our mobile app) at the counter when placing your order. Our staff will apply all applicable discounts and benefits automatically.

Are there any locations where my Premium Card isn't valid?

Your Premium Card is valid at all official Ancient Aroma locations. However, some airport or licensed locations may have limited benefits. Check our website for a complete list of participating locations.

How do I upgrade my plan?

You can upgrade your plan at any time through your online account or by visiting any Ancient Aroma location. When upgrading, you'll only pay the difference between your current plan and the new one.
